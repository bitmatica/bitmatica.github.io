Bitmatica joins Credit Karma!

Today we’re excited to announce that the Bitmatica team has joined Credit Karma to continue making finance accessible for everyone.

Bitmatica began its journey over nine years ago to help organizations worldwide build great products. We believe the best products come from an incredible team who care deeply about every single detail, from each customer it serves to every line of code it’s powered by.

Our passion for great products and even greater outcomes has allowed us to collaborate with leading brands in financial services, healthcare, security, logistics, manufacturing, and much more.

We realized that while each industry has its unique challenges, Bitmatica’s secret sauce is universally applicable –

  • A top-tier team of engineers, designers and product leaders
  • An almost singular focus on customer needs
  • Comprehensive, detail-oriented project planning
  • Constant obsession with “why,” not just “how” and “what”
  • Tight feedback loops and organization-wide collaboration

After countless products shipped, we’re proud to have set a standard for building successful startups inside major organizations. And as we look to the future, we think about how we can leverage our team and process for even greater impact.

Since 2017, Credit Karma has been one of our most valued partners. Credit Karma has always shared our passion for building exceptional products with a laser focus on the impact they deliver to their members, and for years our teams have worked together as one to build technology that makes finance easier for consumers.

But this is just the beginning. We’re thrilled to combine Bitmatica’s execution expertise with Credit Karma’s scale of more than 100 million members to redefine what it means to turn financial dreams into reality.

Together, we will be furthering our previous work in building momentum for Credit Karma’s first-of-its-kind enterprise platform Lightbox, which allows lenders to leverage thousands of de-identified data points from Credit Karma members so those members have more transparency into their likelihood of approval. With Lightbox, Credit Karma strives to give its members access to the best financial products – by creating a platform where machine-learning is applied to surface the best, most certain offers to fit members’ financial situations.

We’d like to thank every single person who has been a part of Bitmatica’s success over this near decade – our clients, team members, partners, friends, and family. We’ve been absolutely humbled by what we’ve built together, and we hope you’ll stay tuned for what comes next.

We know you have your choice of incredible journeys. We appreciate you spending this one with us.

– Matt, Cody, Carl, and the Bitmatica team
